# To Change Car 

- Good Points 
    - Car value now - 80,000 in 2026, will reduce Year by year
    - If we buy EV car we have Government rebate - 7,500, no more after 2026. 
    - BYD Free 10 years battery warranty. 
- Bad Points
    - BYD EV Car resale value is low after 4 years. 
    - EV Car Charging is not so convenient, BYD takes 25 mins to charge. 
    - EV Car Insurance is higher. 
    - Battery replacement can be very high 30k to 40k.
